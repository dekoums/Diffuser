Cleaning your LETTE Diffuser

For best results with your LETTE Diffuser, we recommend cleaning it after each use.

Before you clean your LETTE Diffuser, you will need water, mild soap, and a soft cloth towel.
To clean your diffuser, follow these instructions:
  1. Unplug the AC adaptor from the electrical outlet and from the side of your diffuser.
  2. Remove the water tank lid and empty out any remaining water.
  3. Place your diffuser on a flat surface.
  4. Add a small amount of water and mild liquid soap in the water tank and use a soft cloth towel to gently clean the water tank and any other soiled areas outside of the water tank.
    CAUTION: Do not use harsh cleaning chemicals or abrasive materials as this can damage your LETTE Diffuser.
  5. Rinse out the water tank and dry off any access water.
  6. Place the water tank lid back on top of your diffuser.
Your LETTE Diffuser is clean and ready to be put to work again!
