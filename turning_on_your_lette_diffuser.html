Turning on your LETTE Diffuser

Awaken your senses and enjoy the benefits of aromatherapy with your LETTE Diffuser.

Before turning on your diffuser, you will need room temperature water and essential oil(s).
To turn on your diffuser, follow these instructions:
  1. Place your diffuser on a flat surface and remove the water tank lid.
  2. Use the provided measuring cup to fill the water tank to the “MAX fill” line with room temperature water.
    CAUTION: Do not overfill the water tank as this can damage your LETTE Diffuser.
  3. Add 4-6 drops of your favorite essential oil(s) and place the water tank lid back on top of your diffuser.
  4. Plug the head of the AC adaptor into the power jack on the side of your diffuser and plug the other end into an electrical outlet.
  5. Press the Mist button to turn on your diffuser.
    For safety, your diffuser will shut off automatically when water runs out of the water tank.
Your LETTE Diffuser will do the rest. Sit back and enjoy the aroma!
